Welcome to T.A.P.P.S.

The mission of TAPPS. (Trumpeters Alliance to Perform Patriotic Services) is to perform "Taps" at military funerals and other patriotic events throughout the Las Vegas valley. The primary purpose of this organization is to give back one last time to those who chose to serve our nation in the United States military.
